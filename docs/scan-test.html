📷 Camera scan test

A throwaway test — does your iPhone read a product barcode with the camera? Nothing is saved or uploaded; this isn't the real app.

How to test:

  1. Tap Start camera and Allow the camera prompt.
  2. Point the back camera at any product barcode (a grocery item, a book…).
  3. Hold steady — a green box with the number means it worked. ✅

Best test: also tap Share → Add to Home Screen, open it from the icon, and try again — that's the “installed app” mode that matters.

Ready. Tap “Start camera”.
